Tijaniya Guide

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Tijaniya Guide ("the App") is developed and operated by PetalKube. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

  • Location — Your device location (GPS coordinates) is used solely to calculate accurate prayer times for your area. Your location is processed on-device and is never transmitted to or stored on our servers.
  • Microphone & Audio Recordings — Admin users may record voice announcements using the microphone. These recordings are uploaded to our secure servers and shared within the community. The microphone is never accessed without your explicit action.
  • Photos & Images — The App accesses your photo library to allow uploading of profile photos, payment screenshots, and content images. Images are stored securely on our servers and used only within the App.
  • Account Information — When you register, we collect your name and email address to manage your community membership and authenticate you within the App.
  • Push Notification Token — A device token is collected to send you prayer time reminders and community notifications. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

How We Use Your Information

All information collected is used exclusively to provide and improve features within the Tijaniya Guide app for the Tijaniya Islamic community. Specifically:

  • Location is used to calculate and display local prayer times.
  • Audio recordings are used to share voice announcements within the community.
  • Images are used for profiles, community content, and payment verification.
  • Account information is used to authenticate users and manage memberships.
  • Notification tokens are used to deliver timely prayer and community alerts.

We do not sell, rent, share, or use your data for advertising or any purpose outside the App.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (supabase.com), a trusted cloud infrastructure provider. Data is retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the address below.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — Database and file storage (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Google Firebase — Push notifications (firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
  • Sentry — Crash reporting, with personally identifiable information scrubbed (sentry.io/privacy)

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by members of the Tijaniya Islamic community and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
